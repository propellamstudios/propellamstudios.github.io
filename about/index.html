Exploring the world of traditional and folk music

Hi, I’m Joyce! I’m an amateur musician who plays the violin (decently), sings (mediocrely), and plays the guitar (poorly), and this website is where I’m recording my exploration of the world of traditional and folk music. The music that I jam to and listen to is mostly bluegrass and old-time, but I’m looking to explore other types of traditional and folk music, e.g. Irish, Swedish, English, blues, Cajun.
